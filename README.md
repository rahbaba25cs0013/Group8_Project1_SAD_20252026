## 2. Project Overview

The **Smart Student Printing Service** is a web/mobile-based system designed to 
simplify the campus printing experience for students. It allows students to:

- Upload documents online from anywhere
- Choose printing settings (color, page size, copies, etc.)
- View estimated cost before confirming
- Make cashless digital payments
- Avoid long queues at campus printing shops

---

## 3. Objectives

- Reduce waiting time at campus printing facilities
- Enable online document submission and printing configuration
- Support cashless payment for printing services
- Improve overall efficiency of campus printing operations

---

## 4. Scope

**Included:**
- Online document upload and print job submission
- Printing options selection (B&W/color, single/double-sided, copies etc etc)
- Price estimation before order confirmation
- Digital cashless payment integration (TnG)
- Staff dashboard to manage and fulfill print orders

**Not Included:**
- Off-campus printing services
- Physical printer hardware modifications
- Cash payment handling
- Non-academic print jobs
